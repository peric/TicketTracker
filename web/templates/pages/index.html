About TicketTracker

TicketTracker is full featured agile development tracker which used in real business daily and that can fit well for small/mid size business. Plan your sprints, assign tasks, put people on fire, and get things done!

Features

Built using open technologies, available on github, feel free to fork it!

  • Sprints, tasks and fire support.
  • Easy-peasy drag-n-drop tasks management
  • Burndown chart
  • Works on iPhone, iPad and Desktops without experience loss
